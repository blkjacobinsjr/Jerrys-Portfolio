Overview

iOS App / AI Platform

Role: Co-Founder

The native iOS companion to the Scenaryo web app. Chat with fictional characters who remember you, maintain boundaries, and require you to earn their trust through dialogue. Features text and voice chat, customizable tone settings, AI model selection, scene rewinding, group chats with crossover scenarios, and relationship progress tracking with Ship XP. Private by design with a consent-forward approach.
