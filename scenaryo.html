Scenaryo

Where your story meets theirs

Overview

Web App / AI Platform

Role: Co-Founder

The evolution of SceneXtras into a full-fledged web application. Scenaryo lets users challenge and chat with popular movie, series, anime, and cartoon characters powered by AI. Enter mid-conflict, earn their trust, and shape the story. Features include character creation tools, community content, and deep conversational AI that stays true to each character's personality and story arc.
